Chocolate Fat Bomb

Description

These delicious chocolate fat bombs are packed with 8.2g of fat and only 1.6g of net carbs per serving, perfect for the ketogenic diet.

Try this no-bake recipe! You won't miss store-bought chocolate anymore as these fat bombs offer a similar taste and mouthfeel.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup nut butter of choice
  • 1/4 cup cocoa or cacao powder
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • stevia to taste, optional
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt, optional

Steps

  1. Stir all ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Pour into ice cube trays or mini cupcake tins.
  3. Freeze to set.
  4. Store in freezer.
